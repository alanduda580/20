FIVE. FIVE DOLLAR. FIVE DOLLAR FOOTLONG.
2008
You've all heard it. This was the year. We worked with Subway for over 10 years and the $5 Footlong campaign was one of the reasons they went from a sandwich chain to the #2 QSR in the world. The $5 Footlong campaign alone was estimated to net Subway $4 billion in profits, that's billion with a B.
